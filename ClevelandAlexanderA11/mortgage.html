Enter the amount of the mortgage, change the yearly interest rate in percentage (e.g. 8.5% = 8.5) if you are quoted a different rate and if you are quoted a different rate, and select length of loan in number of years. Click the "calculate" button to view the monthly payment. Click "reset" button to clear the form. For personal and business loans there will be a $1000.00 and $500.00 origination fees that will be included in monthly payments. There is no origination fee for education loans.
